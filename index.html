Willies Widdle Workshop

I specialize in wood working.

Inspiration

I am nothing without the people who helped me get to where I am today, especially my grandparents who let me work in their shop when I was younger. Being able to carry on a form of woodworking using my grandfather’s tools means everything to me, and I wish to share the joy it brings me by creating things for others.

What I Do, Where I Work

I currently have a full-time job, but in my free time after work, I spend time woodworking in my grandparents' garage, fulfilling unique and challenging requests from friends and family. To see what I’ve created so far, check out my collection below!
